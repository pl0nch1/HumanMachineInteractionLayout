University Life

Clubs and Organizations

Joining a club or organization is a great way to meet new people, develop new skills, and enhance your academic life. Explore our wide range of clubs and find your community at Emperor Alexander I St. Petersburg State Transport University.

Events

Our university is home to vibrant events all year round, from academic conferences to cultural festivals. These events provide opportunities for students to engage, learn, and have fun. Stay updated with our events calendar and don't miss out on the excitement.

Sports and Recreation

Sports play a crucial role in university life, offering a balanced educational experience for our students. Participate in our sports programs to keep active, compete, and represent our university. Check out our sports teams and facilities.
